Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What formats are accepted for submission? ⌄

Only .pdf or .doc files are accepted. Ensure your file is final and formatted correctly.

What happens after I submit my work? ⌄

Our editorial team will review your file. You’ll be contacted if revisions are needed. Once approved, we proceed to publication.

Can I edit my book after publishing? ⌄

Yes. Use the Author Dashboard to request updates to your book or dissertation.

Can publishers upload multiple books at once? ⌄

Yes. If you need help contact our support team for bulk upload assistance.

Can students earn royalties on dissertations? ⌄

Yes, dissertations are treated as standard publications. Royalties apply on sales.

Still Have Questions?

We’re here to help. Reach out to our friendly support team for anything you need—from technical support to publishing guidance.
